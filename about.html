About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the team behind Sierra Leone's premier football agency

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Football Agency Sierra Leone began with a simple mission: to provide Sierra Leonean football talent with the professional representation they deserve. What started as a small agency with just 5 players has grown into the country's leading football management company.

Over the past decade, we've facilitated over 25 international transfers, helping Sierra Leonean players reach clubs in Europe, Asia, and across Africa. Our success is built on trust, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to our players' careers.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving Sierra Leonean football talent to global recognition

Our Mission

To professionally represent and develop Sierra Leonean football talent, providing comprehensive career management that maximizes opportunities both domestically and internationally. We strive to be the bridge between local talent and global football markets.

Our Vision

To establish Sierra Leone as a recognized hub for football talent in Africa, where every gifted player has access to professional representation, development pathways, and opportunities to compete at the highest levels of world football.
